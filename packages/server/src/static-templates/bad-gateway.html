502

Bad Gateway

The server encountered a temporary error and could not complete your request.

What happened?
  • The upstream service is currently unavailable or unreachable
  • The job action may have failed to respond in time
  • There may be a temporary network connectivity issue
What can I do?
  • Wait a moment and try your request again
  • Check if the job and its action are properly configured
  • Review the job logs for more detailed error information
  • Contact your administrator if the problem persists
